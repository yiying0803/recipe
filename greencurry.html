Ingredients:

  • 4-6 tablespoons Thai Green Curry Paste
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 cup (250ml) chicken or vegetable broth, low sodium
  • 400g/14oz coconut milk (full fat)
  • 1-3 teaspoons fish sauce (to taste)
  • 1-3 teaspoons white sugar (to taste)
  • 6 kaffir lime leaves (torn in half)
  • 350g/12oz chicken thigh (skinless boneless, sliced)
  • 2 eggplants
  • 1 ½ cups snow peas
  • 16 thai basil leaves
  • Juice of ½ a lime

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a heavily based skillet or pot over medium high heat.
  2. Add curry paste and cook for 2-3 minutes until it mostly dries out.
  3. Add chicken broth and coconut milk, mix to dissolve paste.
  4. Add in fish sauce, sugar, salt.
  5. Add kaffir lime leaves, mix and bring to simmer
  6. Add chicken, stir, then lower heat to medium so it bubbles gently. Cook for 7 minutes
  7. Add eggplants, cook for 5 minutes or until soft
  8. Add snow peas, cook 2 minutes until a bit softened, then stir through basil and lime juice. Sauce should have reduced, but will still be on the thin not thick side. Do not keep simmering – sauce will darken
  9. Serve with rice
